# Contributing to SC-900 Study Guide

Thank you for your interest in contributing to this SC-900 study guide!

## About Cloud360 Training

### Our Mission
To make Microsoft certification preparation accessible, affordable, and effective for everyone.

### Our Values
- **Quality**: Accurate, up-to-date content
- **Accessibility**: Free and open resources
- **Community**: Collaborative learning
- **Excellence**: Expert instruction and guidance

## How to Contribute

We welcome contributions in the following areas:

### 1. Content Improvements
- Fix typos and errors
- Clarify confusing sections
- Add missing information
- Update outdated content

### 2. Resource Links
- Add valuable study resources
- Share useful tutorials
- Recommend helpful tools
- Suggest community content

### 3. Lab Exercises
- Create new lab scenarios
- Improve existing labs
- Add troubleshooting guides
- Share best practices

### 4. Practice Questions
- Contribute practice questions
- Share exam tips
- Add study strategies
- Provide feedback

## Contribution Guidelines

1. **Fork the repository**
2. **Create a feature branch** (`git checkout -b feature/improvement`)
3. **Make your changes**
4. **Commit with clear messages** (`git commit -m 'Add improvement'`)
5. **Push to your fork** (`git push origin feature/improvement`)
6. **Create a Pull Request**

## Code of Conduct

- Be respectful and professional
- Provide constructive feedback
- Focus on quality and accuracy
- Respect intellectual property
- Support the community
